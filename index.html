Where

The Garden. A private, 22 acre enchanted forest estate nestled in the heart of the Lima Valley.

1800sqm [19000sqft] of buildings & infrastructure, featuring established forest, orchards, and groves.

Facilities include a pool, sauna on a private spring fed lake, a 12-person hot tub, natural cold plunge, a granite villa, two grand halls, an embodiment space, four kitchens, sundecks, wooden platforms, a giant dance floor, yoga studio, gym (w/ power rack), dojo & jam space (full of instruments & soundsystems), two aerial silks, lyra, and an arts & crafts zone.
